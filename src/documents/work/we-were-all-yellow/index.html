We Were All Yellow

Media:
oil on canvas
Size:
36" x 36" each
Date:
spring 2007

We Were All Yellow was my senior Honors thesis project on Asian stereotypes in America. I surveyed several Caucasians about what they picture when they think of Asian people. These were the most common answers I was given. It's meant to be light-hearted, poking fun at our cultural differences and perceptions.
