Rynn Fisher Artist & Designer

I came to this great nation when I was only 11 months old and was shot out of a t-shirt cannon across the Pacific. I settled in Scottsdale, Arizona where, in kindergarten, I won my very first art contest. I was off to a good start.

In high school, between pop-punk shows in the Nile basement, I taught myself HTML and made my very first website: a fansite for a local band I stalked, I mean liked.

Later I attended Arizona State University where I slowly decided I didn't want to be a computer animator and discovered a love for painting. I also fell in love with garlic flavored pizza crust, but that's another tale.

In the fall of 2007, I somehow got a job at meltmedia where I now focus my time on designing for the web, making decisions in the browser, and improving communication between designers and developers.

I now live in Chandler with my shy, but insanely funny husband Clay and our two dogs, Boomer and Helo.

Also: chillax, Mom. It's just butterbeer.
