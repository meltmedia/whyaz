whyaz

There's more than desert.

Arizona has an elevation range of over 12.5k feet.

Arizona's Tonto National Park is the 4th largest in the country.

Arizona has 128 lakes, including Lakes Mead, Havasu, and Powell.

We gots the weather.

Phoenix boasts 296 days of sunshine each year.

But if you like snow, Flagstaff averages 100.3 inches annually.

The average temperature in Phoenix is 72.6 degrees.
